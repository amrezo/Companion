    Automatic & Passive

    Companion automatically and passively tracks your mental wellness state and suggests ways to come back to a balanced mood state when you inadvertently divert from it.

    Companion uses the power of machine learning to help suggest and advice on how to bring you back to a balanced mental state. This includes advice on sleep, exercise, meditation and other factors that affect your mental well-being.

    Companion automatically tracks the duration of night-time sleep, exercise, social engagement and medication compliance.
